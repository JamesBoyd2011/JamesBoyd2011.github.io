Telelphone Distribution Wiring

In a multi dwelling family unit, the telephone company is responsible for bringing the dial tone for the residents phone service up to what is known as the DMARC or Demarcation. The DMARC is the telephone company’s main telephone terminal that may be in the same building as the customer or in adjacent buildings. From this point, the customer is responsible for hiring another company to extend their telephone service into the customers apartment/condo. When the telephone service is extended from the DMARC, the distribution wiring also called house cabling which is owned by the Apartment/Condo is utilized to complete the wiring of the phone service. In some cases the wiring is very old, damaged or is otherwise not adequate to support the functionality of the telephone line. That’s where we come in. Our company can evaluate your existing distribution wiring infrastructure and provide you with a comprehensive, cost effective method to repair or replace the damaged areas of the telephone distribution wiring on your property.

We can provide; ·An entire replacement of the old telephone terminals and wiring from the telephone company DMARC ·A detailed custom solution to repair the most damaged sections of your distribution wiring infrastructure ·A single tenant solution to replace the existing wiring for a resident that needs their wiring repaired or extended from the telephone company’s DMARC to the customers residence
